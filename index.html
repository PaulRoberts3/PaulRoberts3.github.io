About Us

The mission of Minneola Charter School Mustang Band is to unlock the creativity and ability that all students possess in order to develop the well rounded leaders of tomorrow. The Band was founded in August 2018 and offers Beginning Band through Advanced Band instruction on modern wind band instruments.
